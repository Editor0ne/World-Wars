WW1 Summary

It lasted from July 29, 1914, to November 11, 1918, and led to more than 70 million military personnel being deployed throughout the world.
It was one of the largest wars in history and also one of the deadliest.
Nine million combatants and seven million civilians died as a direct result of the war.
